# Code of conduct

## Our standards

Contributors must communicate respectfully, welcome constructive review, and focus discussion on the work. Harassment, discrimination, threats, personal attacks, deliberate disruption, and disclosure of another person's private information are unacceptable.

Protect participant privacy and dataset restrictions. Never use community spaces to share participant records, credentials, restricted content, or sensitive local information.

## Scope

These standards apply in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other project spaces, as well as when representing the project publicly.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove contributions and restrict participation when conduct is unsafe, abusive, irrelevant, or inconsistent with these standards. Concerns should be reported privately through the process in [Security](SECURITY.md) when public discussion would expose sensitive information.

Maintainers will seek a proportionate, fair response while prioritising participant privacy and community safety.
